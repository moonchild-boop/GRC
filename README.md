## Approach

My work is guided by a simple philosophy:

> Technical findings should inform business decisions.

Whether identifying vulnerabilities within a laboratory environment, conducting risk assessments, developing security policies, or mapping organizational controls to industry frameworks, my focus remains the same:

1. Identify the problem.
2. Understand the organizational impact.
3. Assess the associated risks.
4. Recommend appropriate security controls.
5. Align recommendations with recognized frameworks and standards.
6. Promote sustainable and secure organizational practices.

This repository reflects my approach to cybersecurity as both a technical and strategic discipline that connects people, processes, technology, and governance.
